From concept
to hardware.

I'm a UCLA Mechanical Engineering student interested in the full engineering loop: defining a problem, building a model, designing the hardware, manufacturing the parts, testing the result, and improving the next version.

My work spans motorsport engineering with Bruin Formula Racing, aerospace manufacturing experience, MEMS hardware, biomedical flow research, robotics leadership, and product design through my 3D printing business.

I like projects where analysis and fabrication meet — suspension geometry that has to package cleanly, fixtures that have to survive real use, sensors that need to validate data, and robots that have to work on the field when the clock starts.
