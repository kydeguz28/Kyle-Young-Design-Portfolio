Robotics Project

FRC Shooter Mechanism

Led mechanical design and subsystem integration for a competition robot shooter at SF Unity Robotics. The project centered on delivering consistent accuracy and rapid serviceability in a six-week build season.

I worked through multiple prototype iterations, selected actuation hardware, and coordinated mounting and belt-routing to support reliable performance under match conditions. The design also had to fit within strict FRC robot packaging and weight limitations.

The final assembly balanced shooting consistency with maintainability so the team could tune and repair the mechanism quickly during events.
