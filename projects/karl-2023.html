FRC Robot

Karl

Karl was the 2023 FRC Charged Up robot, and I helped develop its low-profile geometry, scoring mechanism layout, and drivetrain packaging. The team needed a compact robot that could score reliably and recover quickly during matches.

I worked on packaging the scoring mechanism within the 125 lb robot frame, arranging belts and motors for consistent actuation, and designing the structure so repairs could be made quickly between cycles. The design process included rapid prototyping and close collaboration with fabricators.

Karl taught me how to make tough tradeoffs between performance, durability, and manufacturability in a short season, and reinforced my approach to designing field-ready robotics hardware.
