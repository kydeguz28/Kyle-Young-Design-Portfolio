FRC Robot

Karl

Karl was the 2023 FRC Charged Up robot, and I helped develop the robot's low-profile geometry, scoring mechanism layout, and drivetrain reliability.

The design work emphasized manufacturability and robustness in a fast-paced build season, with a focus on ensuring the robot could handle repeated cycles and repairs between matches.

Through Karl, I gained practical experience in FRC robot architecture, subsystem integration, and designing within the constraints of weight, size, and competition rules.
