FRC Robot

Karl

Karl was SF Unity's 2023 FRC Charged Up robot, and the team's first. We took the everybot design and adapted it for our team, changing the drivebase and made improvements towards the weaknesses of the original design, which was cg height and slower scoring. The team needed a compact robot that could score reliably and recover quickly during matches.

I worked on leading fabrication and assembly of the robot, as well as administratively coordinating build season schedule, sponsors/grants, and building out organizational infrastructure with a buisness plan and DEI plan for the team.

The team qualified for the FIRST World Championship, and Karl taught me that sometimes simpler is better, keep it simple, stupid!
