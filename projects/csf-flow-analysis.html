Biomedical Research

CSF Flow Analysis

Built Python workflows to extract, clean, compare, and visualize cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) flow features from medical imaging datasets. This work supported a research collaboration focused on noninvasive assessment of Chiari malformation surgical outcomes.

I processed raw data, implemented signal-cleaning routines, and generated visual analysis that helped the team understand how flow dynamics changed before and after surgical intervention. The work also included documenting patterns and building reproducible analysis pipelines.

The project combined research-focused engineering with data-driven storytelling and helped translate medical fluid dynamics into actionable insights for the research team.
