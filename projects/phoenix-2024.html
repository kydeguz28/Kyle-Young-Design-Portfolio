FRC Robot

Phoenix

Phoenix was the 2024 FRC Crescendo competition robot. I helped design key subsystems and integrate the robot architecture so the team could execute a game strategy focused on consistent scoring and rapid defensive resets.

My contributions included chassis packaging, intake/shooter layout, and robust cable routing. I worked to ensure the robot's subsystems cleared each other through the full range of motion and that all components were accessible for quick repairs during events.

The design emphasized reliability and field durability, with repeatable mechanisms and mounting details that reduced setup time between matches. Phoenix reflects my ability to design competition-grade hardware under tight time constraints.
