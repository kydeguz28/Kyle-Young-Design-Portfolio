FRC Robot

Phoenix

Phoenix was the 2024 FRC competition robot built for the Crescendo game. I contributed to chassis packaging, shooter and intake subsystem integration, and mechanical design decisions that shaped the robot's performance.

My work focused on ensuring reliable actuation, robust cable routing, and efficient use of the robot volume so the drive system and scoring mechanisms could operate without interference. The project emphasized durability and quick serviceability during event cycles.

Phoenix demonstrates my experience designing competitive robotics hardware under season constraints while supporting a team-focused build workflow.
