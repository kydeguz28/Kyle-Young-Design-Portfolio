Formula SAE Project

Anti-Roll Bar Test Fixture

Designed and built a mechanical fixture to validate anti-roll bar stiffness and mounting behavior. The project translated a vehicle dynamics question into a controlled bench test capable of producing repeatable engineering data.

I specified loading fixtures, selected test hardware, and developed the physical layout so the system could clamp to the bar, apply displacement, and measure response with high fidelity. The fixture design prioritized ease of use, modularity, and compatibility with the bars used on the Formula SAE car.

This project improved our understanding of anti-roll bar validation and supported data-driven design choices for suspension tuning across multiple track conditions.
